# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Participation in the Selflify community should be respectful, professional, and constructive.

Examples of expected behavior:

- being respectful in discussions and reviews
- focusing on technical substance instead of personal attacks
- accepting feedback in good faith
- helping keep issues and pull requests actionable

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- harassment, insults, or discriminatory language
- trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- publishing someone else's private information
- bad-faith escalation in issues, PRs, or discussions

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, issues, pull requests, discussions, or contributions that violate this code of conduct.

## Reporting

If you need to report unacceptable behavior, contact the maintainers privately through GitHub.
